Weekly Thrills at Skate Shack

Event Schedule Explained

At Skate Shack, we believe that every day should be an adventure on wheels, and our weekly event schedule reflects that spirit! From Monday to Sunday, we've curated a lineup of activities to keep the skatepark alive with energy and excitement. Let's dive into the heart of the action, especially highlighting the adrenaline-packed weekend events that define the Skate Shack experience.

Overview:

Mon - Thur

  • Open Session: 9am - 4pm
  • Booked Lessons: 4pm - 7pm
  • Open Session: 7pm - 10pm

Friday

  • Open Session: 9am - 10pm

Saturday

  • Open Session: 9am - 3pm
  • Weekly Jam: 3pm - 5pm
  • Weekly Competition: 5pm - 7pm
  • Open Session: 7pm - 10pm

Sunday

  • Open Session: 9am - 4pm

Monday to Thursday: Roll into Routine

Kickstart your weekdays with our open sessions from 9 am to 4 pm, perfect for honing your skills or trying out new tricks. For those looking to elevate their game, booked lessons are available from 4 pm to 7 pm. Cap off the evening with another round of open sessions from 7 pm to 10 pm. It's your playground, and the possibilities are endless.

Friday: Unleash the Weekend Vibe

Friday at Skate Shack is all about freedom. Enjoy an extended open session from 9 am to 10 pm, giving you the flexibility to ride the ramps and rails at your own pace. Start your weekend early and make every trick count!

Saturday: Jam, Compete, and Conquer

Saturdays are a skater's dream. Begin the day with an open session from 9 am to 3 pm. As the afternoon unfolds, join the Weekly Jam from 3 pm to 5 pm, where the park comes alive with a fusion of styles and skills. The excitement continues with the Weekly Competition from 5 pm to 7 pm, offering a platform for skaters to showcase their best moves. Round off the day with another open session from 7 pm to 10 pm, giving you the chance to relive the highlights or conquer new challenges!

Sunday: Cruise into Relaxation

Wrap up the week with a laid-back Sunday session. Our open skate runs from 9 am to 4 pm, providing a leisurely space to cruise, chill, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow skaters.
